The Eight Agents
🎯 Oprah-tor Frontier
Orchestrator. Decomposes requests, dispatches the team, validates outputs, synthesizes the final package.
🔍 Sherlock Ohms Frontier
Executive Research. Deep person-level intelligence: career arc, recent activity, communication style.
🏢 Bloom-borg Frontier
Account Intelligence. Company-level analysis: financials, tech stack, competitive landscape.
📋 Déjà View Local
SAB Historian. Cross-program memory: SAB membership, past briefings, engagement stage, open follow-ups.
📄 Draft Punk Local
Briefing Architect. Document assembly: dossier, backgrounder, talking points, agenda, conversation starters.
⭐ Alfred Bitworth Local
VVIP Protocol. Tier verification, checklist generation, sponsor alerts, scheduling, engagement logging.
🤝 Sponsor Coach New
Readiness Check. Assesses whether the assigned sponsor is prepared for their role. Recommends a single pre-briefing action.
🎲 The Oddsfather New
Success Predictor. Final probability score on briefing outcome. Five-dimensional scoring with the three actions that move the odds.
